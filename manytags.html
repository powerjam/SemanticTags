work

When I graduated, I went straight into a Big 4 firm in IT audit/risk/compliance/whatever you want to call it. I was essentially an external auditor; the kind of person most workers hate. I learned quickly that it was not the right career fit for me.

After a couple of years in IT compliance, I accepted a role at Boeing as a Systems Analyst. I've only been there 2.5 years, but I've had a few roles since I joined: Functional Analyst, Product Owner, and now UX Designer.

I really enjoy what I'm doing currently as UX Designer, which I'm still learning a lot as I go. We are working on a maintenance data delivery project where we are completely redesigning current systems into an entirely new website. I hope this certificate program will come in handy since we want our website to be modern and responsive for mobile access.
